We Beat It, You Eat It!

Edible Delights opened it's doors on March 20th, 2020. Our mission is to provide small, healthy and fresh treats to our community. We also offer larger custom cakes for celebrations - please see our Order page for details.

Treat yourself! People don't normally think of cakes as being both healthy and tasty - we can change your mind! We bake everything from scratch with fresh ingredients (local when possible). Our products are vegan (dairy and egg free), gluten free, peanut free, soy free and we avoid using processed sugars and perservatives. The flours we use include garbanzo bean/chickpea flour and brown rice flour. These wheat alternatives provide more fiber and protein - you can feel good about eating them.

Fresh Fresh Baby! We make small batches of our Daily Treats everyday to make sure they are always fresh. Beacuse of this you should call ahead of time to order large quanities. In order to be able to do this we also have a limited menu.


About the Owner :)

Tiffany graduated from The Restaurant School in Philadelphia with a Pastry Arts degree in 2010. She has worked at several different bakeries including Sohpisticakes, Viso's and Traubs. This helped her fine tune her decorating skills and knowledge of how bakeries run. She opened Edible Delights with the goal of fixing problems she noticed at previous bakeries.
